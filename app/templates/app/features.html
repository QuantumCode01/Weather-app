Welcome to our Weather App!

Our Weather App is a powerful tool built using Python and Django, designed to provide you with accurate and up-to-date weather information. Powered by the OpenWeather API, it fetches real-time weather data from around the world and presents it to you in a user-friendly manner.

Key Features:

  1. Current Weather Conditions: Get instant access to the current weather conditions in any location. Our app retrieves data such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and atmospheric pressure , ensuring you have all the necessary information at your fingertips.
  2. Forecasting: Stay prepared with our reliable weather forecasts. Our app provides detailed forecasts for the next 5 days,with data every 3 hours by city name giving you a clear understanding of what to expect in terms of temperature changes, humidity, and more.
